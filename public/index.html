The AQI

The AQI, or Air Quality Index, is an indicator of cleaniness in the air. The higher the index, the worse the air quality. It ranges from 0 to 500.

0-50 Good
51-100 Moderate
101-150Unhealthy for Sensitive Groups
151-200 Unhealthy
201-300Very Unhealthy
301-500Harzardous
